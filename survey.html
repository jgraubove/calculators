Understanding Standard Error & $MoE$

The Standard Error ($SE$) measures how much your estimate would vary if the survey were repeated many times. It indicates the statistical precision of your calculated mean or proportion.

The Margin of Error ($MoE$) defines the half-width of the confidence interval around your estimate ($\text{Estimate} \pm MoE$). It is calculated as:

$$MoE = \text{Critical Value } (z \text{ or } t) \times SE$$
